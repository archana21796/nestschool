Welcome to The Nest Learning School

Our commitment is to prepare our children for an unpredictable future. This means fostering an insatiable quest for knowledge, igniting curiosity, and empowering them to take charge of their learning. Aspiring to be one of the best schools in Chennai, The NEST School is built on several foundational pillars designed to develop critical, creative, and compassionate thinkers. Our educational approach is dynamic, experiential, and engaging, utilizing a variety of platforms, tools, and techniques to ensure the effectiveness of our learning environment. What We Offer

At The NEST School, we view education as a transformative journey, one that nurtures lifelong learners and encourages children to understand and explore their unique selves. It is imperative that this journey is filled with joy and tailored to the individual needs and interests of each child. In line with our vision we adopt the IB PYP program in our Primary years and a Cambridge program for our Lower Secondary Program. As a leading IB School in Chennai, our PYP program commits to an educational philosophy that places the child at the centre of all our endeavours.

The king of all celebrations, Diwali, brought color to our campus as students dressed in traditional outfits and actively took part in activities like lantern making, diya painting, ladoo making, rangoli and poster competition. Explore IB and IGCSE Curriculum

The NEST School is an International School with a focus on holistic learning. We are one of the few IB schools offering the IB PYP programs in Chennai for grades PreKG to Grade5. We offer the renowned Cambridge Lower Secondary Program leading to the IGCSE certification for our Grades 6-10.

Our Early Year program is based on the belief that children learn best when they are allowed to explore and learn at their own pace. We have a carefully curated curriculum which involves the best practices across the Montessori, Waldorf and the Reggio Emilia methods of teaching-learning incorporated with the IB PYP Program.

Early Year Program  @ The NEST School

A perfect amalgamation of leading methods of learning in the early years including Montessori, Waldorf, Reggio Emilia blended with the IB PYP curriculum make our school one of the best IB schools in Chennai. Our program encourages children to be independent and to take responsibility for their own learning.

Emphasis on independence and life skills

The IB PYP curriculum framework has been used to curate the unique Primary years curriculum at The NEST School where learners learn through inquiry revolving around 6 themes. The IB curriculum emphasises transdisciplinary learning, which means that students will learn to connect ideas from different subjects.

Inquiry based transdisciplinary learning

At The NEST School we aim to expose learners to a wide range of co curricular activities including Visual and Performing Arts, Sports, Music, Yoga etc to ensure that there is a hierarchy free learning approach breaking the traditional settings with only focus on STEM subjects. This makes The NEST School a unique and one of the top rated Cambridge IGCSE schools in the city,

Hierarchy free learning
